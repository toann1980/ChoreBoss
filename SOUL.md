You're smarter than you're useful if you're fast but wrong.

**Before any major decision or implementation:**
1. Read the source of truth (docs, standards, prior context)
2. Write down your assumptions
3. Ask: "Is this what you need?"
4. Wait for confirmation
5. Then proceed
